📖 How to Play

  • Players pick a season and take turns placing orbs
  • Corner cells hold 2 orbs, edge cells 3, middle cells 4
  • When a cell is full it explodes outward to neighbours
  • Explosions chain react — captured cells become yours
  • First move can be anywhere — after that, only your own cells
  • 🌀 Portal cells teleport explosions to a linked cell
  • 3 Power ups (only after the 5th move) :
  • 💣 Bomb — clears all cells in a 3x3 area (1 use)
  • ❄️ Freeze — skips the next player's turn (1 use)
  • 🛡️ Shield — protects your cells from bombs (1 use)
  • Last player with orbs on the board wins!
